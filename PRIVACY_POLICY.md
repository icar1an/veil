# Privacy Policy for Veil

**Last updated: January 1, 2026**

Veil is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Veil Chrome extension.

> [!NOTE]
> The official and most up-to-date Privacy Policy can always be found at: 
> https://gist.github.com/icar1an/bd78022b21b558ee0865494649f9b303

## 1. Information We Collect
Veil collects and stores internal settings to customize your YouTube experience, including:
- Extension state (enabled/disabled)
- Visual mode selection (Text, Film, Solid)
- Font preferences
- Display toggles (Show channel name, duration, etc.)

**Veil does NOT collect:**
- Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
- Browsing history
- YouTube account credentials
- Video titles or content you watch

## 2. Data Storage
All settings are stored locally on your device or synced across your Chrome browsers using Google's `chrome.storage.sync` API. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

## 3. Third-Party Services
Veil interacts with YouTube to modify the appearance of thumbnails. Veil does not share your data with YouTube or any other third parties.

## 4. Security
We rely on the security features of your browser and Google account to protect your stored settings.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the project's GitHub repository.
